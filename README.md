## Overview

This is my winter-themed website, designed with a minimalist South Park-inspired aesthetic. The scene features a snowy forest under a starry night sky with a glowing crescent moon. A custom geometric logo appears in the center, blending soft pastel tones. At the bottom, there are transparent icons linking to Discord, Steam, Instagram, and Twitter. Kenny stands on the right side of the screen.
